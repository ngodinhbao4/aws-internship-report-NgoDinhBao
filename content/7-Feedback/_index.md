# Survey & Personal Feedback on the First Cloud Journey (FCJ) Program

### General Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The learning and working environment at the FCJ program is highly collaborative, open, and encourages self-study. Access to modern facilities and a professional setting (such as sessions held at Bitexco) has helped me quickly adapt to the corporate pace. To further optimize, the program could organize more mini-games or brief networking sessions between different project teams to foster greater overall cohesion.

**2. Mentor and Admin Support**  
The mentor team possesses deep technical expertise and utilizes modern pedagogical methods. Instead of providing immediate answers, they consistently guide us through Socratic questioning, encouraging trainees to practice hands-on, identify bugs independently, and take responsibility for our own solutions. The Team Admin is always supportive, handles procedures promptly, and provides comprehensive technical documentation, creating the best possible conditions for trainees.

**3. Alignment with Academic Major**  
The internship program content is perfectly aligned with and strongly supplements my Information Technology / Software Engineering major at university. Academic knowledge is transformed into practical use cases (such as Microservices architecture, Event-Driven design, or infrastructure programming with Terraform), helping me bridge the gap between theory and enterprise practice.

**4. Learning Opportunities & Skill Development**  
This internship has been a major milestone for my hard and soft skill development. Technically, I have been exposed to advanced concepts such as Agentic DevOps, Private Link security, and cloud system optimization. Regarding service skills, I have cultivated a Solution Architect mindset, developed critical questioning (Q&A) techniques, and learned how to work effectively within teams with clearly defined responsibilities.

**5. Culture & Team Spirit**  
The culture of FCJ is very civilized, respects diversity, and emphasizes proactivity. Despite being an intern, my opinions are always heard and respected during solution discussions. When facing difficult problems or high-pressure Hackathons, the team spirit and boundless support between members are major driving forces helping the team achieve its goals.

**6. Benefits & Welfare for Trainees**  
The program has clear, transparent policies and provides the opportunity to participate in high-quality internal training sessions and major Community Day events free of charge—an invaluable benefit for a technology student.

### Suggestions & Future Aspirations
*   **Future Aspirations:** I am very keen on continuing my journey with the First Cloud Journey program, either as an official member or as a Core Member supporting future generations of students.
*   **Additional Feedback:** I would like to express my sincere gratitude to the FCJ team, speakers, and mentors who have dedicated so much heart and weekend time to building a high-quality, completely free technology platform for the student community.
